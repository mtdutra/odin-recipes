Homemade Lemonade

Description

A simple and refreshing homemade lemonade made with freshly squeezed lemons and just the right amount of sweetness. Perfect for hot days or whenever you need a burst of freshness.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4–6 lemons)
  • ½ cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices and mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in one cup of warm water.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the lemon juice, sugar water, and remaining cold water.
  3. Stir well and taste — adjust sweetness if needed.
  4. Add ice cubes before serving.
  5. Garnish with lemon slices and mint leaves if desired.
